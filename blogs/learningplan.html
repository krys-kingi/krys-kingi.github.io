Foundations: Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long term goal is to be able to work remotely and to have the flexibility to possibly work and travel, whilst consistently learning and growing. Another goal of mine is to represent Maori and Pasefika Women in the tech industry. However, in regards to a pathway, I have thought about possibly dabbling with design as well and either look into UX design or possibly even graphic design. I believe that one of these additional skills will add another cool skill to my belt.
